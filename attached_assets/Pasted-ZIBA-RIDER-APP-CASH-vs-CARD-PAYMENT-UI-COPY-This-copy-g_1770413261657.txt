ZIBA RIDER APP — CASH vs CARD PAYMENT UI COPY

This copy governs ALL rider-facing text related to payment method selection.
Tone must be simple, respectful, and neutral.
Do NOT reference platform fees or driver earnings percentages.

====================================
PAYMENT METHOD SELECTION SCREEN
====================================

Title:
“Choose how you want to pay”

Options:

• Card / Wallet
  “Pay securely in the app”

• Cash
  “Pay the driver directly in cash”

Helper text (below options):
“You can change your payment method before the trip starts.”

====================================
CASH PAYMENT — CONFIRMATION SCREEN
====================================

Title:
“Cash payment selected”

Message:
“You’ll pay the driver the full trip amount in cash at the end of the ride.”

Small helper text:
“Please have the correct amount ready.”

====================================
CARD PAYMENT — CONFIRMATION SCREEN
====================================

Title:
“Card payment selected”

Message:
“Your payment will be handled securely in the app.”

====================================
DURING TRIP — PAYMENT REMINDER (CASH)
====================================

Banner (subtle):
“Payment method: Cash”

Small text:
“Please pay the driver directly at the end of the trip.”

====================================
END OF TRIP — CASH PAYMENT SCREEN
====================================

Title:
“Pay your driver”

Amount display:
“Trip total: ₦X / $X”

Instruction:
“Please pay this amount directly to the driver.”

Confirmation button:
“I’ve paid the driver”

====================================
END OF TRIP — CARD PAYMENT SCREEN
====================================

Title:
“Trip completed”

Message:
“Your payment has been processed in the app.”

====================================
RECEIPT — CASH TRIP
====================================

Receipt label:
“Payment method: Cash”

Receipt note:
“This trip was paid directly to the driver.”

====================================
RECEIPT — CARD TRIP
====================================

Receipt label:
“Payment method: Card”

Receipt note:
“Payment processed in the app.”

====================================
HELP CENTER — RIDER FAQ COPY
====================================

Question:
“What’s the difference between cash and card payments?”

Answer:
“With cash payments, you pay the driver directly at the end of the trip.
With card payments, payment is handled securely in the app.
Both options are supported for your convenience.”

====================================
IMPORTANT COPY RULES (LOCKED)
====================================

• Never mention platform fees
• Never mention driver percentages
• Never compare cash vs card pricing
• Never imply drivers earn less with cash
• Keep instructions simple and respectful

====================================
FINAL COPY PRINCIPLE
====================================

Riders should clearly understand:
• Who to pay
• When to pay
• How much to pay

Without ever seeing platform economics.
