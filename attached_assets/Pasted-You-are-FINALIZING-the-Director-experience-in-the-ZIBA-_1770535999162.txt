PHASE 1 — DIRECTOR ONBOARDING FLOW
================================================

1) DIRECTOR ONBOARDING ENTRY

Route:
- /director/onboarding

Triggered when:
- Director is newly appointed
- Director logs in for the first time
- Director status changes from pending → active

Steps:
1. Welcome screen (role explanation)
2. Director responsibilities
3. Driver management rules
4. Commission rules (high-level, no amounts)
5. Staff & permissions rules
6. Compliance & conduct
7. Terms acceptance
8. Dashboard access granted

Director must complete onboarding before full access.

- Staff management best practices
- Avoiding abuse & retaliation
- Understanding trust score
- Working with ZIBRA insights

Rules:
- Training completion logged
- Admin/Super Admin can see completion status
- Refusal to complete → dashboard access limited

================================================
PHASE 3 — DIRECTOR TERMS & CONDITIONS
================================================

3) DIRECTOR TERMS (STORE-READY)

Key points:
- Director is NOT an employee unless explicitly designated
- Director does NOT control pricing or payouts
- Director earns commission only from ZIBA earnings
- Appointment is revocable
- ZIBA may suspend/terminate at discretion
- No guarantee of earnings
- Directors responsible for staff actions
- Dispute process defined
- Governing law configurable per country

Acceptance required before activation.

================================================
PHASE 4 — DIRECTOR TRUST SCORE
================================================

4) TRUST SCORE MODEL (INTERNAL)

Inputs:
- Driver complaints under Director
- Excessive suspensions
- Staff abuse flags
- Missed compliance actions
- ZIBRA risk alerts
- Admin warnings

Outputs:
- Trust score (internal only)
- Risk level: Low / Medium / High

Rules:
- Directors cannot see numeric score
- Admin & Super Admin see full details
- Low trust triggers ZIBRA coaching
- High risk triggers Admin review

================================================
PHASE 5 — ZIBRA COACHING (DIRECTORS)
================================================

5) ZIBRA COACHING PROMPTS

ZIBRA may:
- Suggest performance improvements
- Warn about policy drift
- Encourage compliance
- Highlight positive behavior

ZIBRA must NOT:
- Threaten
- Promise earnings
- Override Admin authority

Examples:
- “You have a higher-than-average suspension rate.”
- “Several drivers under your cell are inactive.”
